About Us

Founded in 2010, Mwangi Wangondu & Associates is a premier audit and advisory firm serving clients across Kenya and East Africa.

Our team of professionals is here to help you navigate financial complexity and grow sustainably.

Who We Are

Mwangi Wangondu & Associates is a registered firm of Certified Public Accountants that offers Audit Assurance and Financial Capacity Development Services with over 15 years of working experience of its key management team across several countries in Africa.

The Firm has undertaken various assignments in Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Zambia and Rwanda. The Firm has a pool of experts drawn from people with excellent knowledge of formulating homegrown solutions to meet emerging challenges in a sustainable way. Our team’s skills draw from long experience in both the local business and development environment and practices, custom and legal requirements by employing international standards and best practices.

In particular, we have a distinction in our management team in service delivery to the public entities companies, government institutions, Cooperative Societies, Private companies, Public Development Programmes and Projects and, the NGO sector.

Our Approach

We collaborate with finance, HR, IT, and legal experts to provide holistic, innovative, and practical solutions tailored to the region’s business challenges.

We undertake statutory financial audits, project audits, feasibility studies, project design, implementation, monitoring and evaluation, capacity building involving fundraising techniques, organizational development, training on accountancy/book keeping and sound financial management, sound procurement policies, proposal writing, staff recruitment, design of budgetary and cost control systems.

We have designed and assisted implement comprehensive System Manuals on Financial Management & Accounting, HR Policies and Procedure Systems and Project Management.

Our Financial Management Agency (FMA) services package cannot fail to be given special mention. In addition, we provide external/ internal auditing and accountancy services at competitive terms and conditions.
